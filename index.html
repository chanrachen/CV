About Me

Today, I would like to talk to you about my passion and career goals. My name is Rachen and I have always had a creative passion since my childhood. I believe that creativity is essential in all of our lives, and it is what drives innovation and progress. I am here today to share with you my aspirations and why I want to become a designer. As a child, I was always interested in art, fashion, and architecture. I spent hours drawing, sketching and imagining my own designs. Over the years, I have honed my skills and have gained knowledge and experience in various areas such as graphic design, fashion design, and interior design. As I have grown older, I have realized that design is not just about creating aesthetically pleasing objects. It is about solving real-world problems and enhancing our lives. Designers have a significant impact on the way we live our lives. Everything around us has been designed, from the buildings we live in, the clothes we wear, the appliances we use, and the websites we browse. Designers make a difference in how we interact with the world, whether that be for the better or for the worse. I want to be a designer because I want to make an impact and contribute to creating meaningful designs that enhance our daily lives. Furthermore, I believe that design is a collaborative process. Designers work with various stakeholders such as clients, users, and other professionals to create designs that meet their needs. This is what attracted me to the field of design. As a designer, I get to work with people from different backgrounds and have the opportunity to learn from them, which broadens my perspective and allows me to create more innovative designs.
